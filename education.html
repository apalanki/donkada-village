Supporting Education

Education provides opportunity to aim for higher standard of living

After 10th class all the students, particularly girls, to travel back and forth nearly 30km and parents hesitate to extent their girls education after 10th. Therefore a problem of dropouts after 10th in girl-students is common phenomenon in this village. By providing transport facility this problem can be solved to some extent. Some colleges are providing bus facility for their students on payment basis. Even so, auto service is needed to provide transport for other girls. 50% of transport cost could be made up by the parents and the remaining share of cost is to be made up by the donors.
